The Collectory

Your collections. Your machine. Nobody else’s business.

A local-first collection manager for firearms, ammunition, magazines, knives, coins, stamps — and anything else worth keeping a proper record of.

Download for macOS

Apple Silicon M1 and later · 99 MB Intel Intel Macs · 105 MB

Not sure which Mac you have? Apple menu → About This Mac. A chip listed as “Apple M-series” is Apple Silicon; anything labeled “Intel” is Intel.

Installing

Open the downloaded disk image, drag The Collectory into your Applications folder, and double-click to open. That’s it — it’s signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple, so it opens like any other app.

Local-first, always

No servers, no cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Your records never leave your Mac unless you export them.

Expert templates

Firearms (incl. NFA & tax-stamp tracking), ammo, knives, coins, stamps — every field editable, plus fully custom collections.

The range loop

Log a session, pick the ammo, and rounds deduct from inventory while round counts and cleaning intervals keep themselves.

Magazines & provenance

Track magazines under each firearm — capacity, loaded state — alongside ownership history and valuation records.

Insurance-ready

A printable report with photos, values, and serials you can mask — structured the way insurers ask for it.

Your iPad, no cloud

Scan a QR code on the same Wi-Fi and add it to your Home Screen. Nothing ever touches the internet.

Plain SQLite and ordinary image files on your own machine. If this app vanished tomorrow, your records would still be yours — readable, portable, complete.
